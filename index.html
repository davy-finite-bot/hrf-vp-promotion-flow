Director → VP Promotion Process

Human Rights Foundation — Internal Promotion Protocol

Define the VP Role
Pre-Process
  • Document VP-level scope, authority, and accountability
  • Clarify what a VP does that a Director doesn't
  • Align title, reporting structure, and compensation band
  • Confirm budget and organizational need
Owner: COO + CEO
1
Establish Readiness Criteria
Pre-Process
  • Build competency framework: leadership, strategic thinking, stakeholder management, talent development
  • Set performance threshold and behavioral evidence requirements
  • Create weighted scorecard with gating categories
  • Define evaluation period and review cadence
Owner: COO + HR
2
Assessment Period
6 Months
  • Monthly 1:1 check-ins with documented evidence
  • Scorecard ratings updated each month
  • 360° peer feedback pulse at Month 3 and Month 6
  • Midpoint review: on track / at risk / off track
  • Track both outcomes delivered and leadership behaviors demonstrated
Owner: COO (evaluator) + Candidate
3
Readiness Review & Recommendation
End of Assessment
  • Final scorecard calculation with weighted scores
  • Check gating categories — no critical area below 3.0
  • Prepare evidence-based recommendation memo
  • Recommendation: Ready Now / Ready Soon / Not Yet Ready
  • Include timeline and any conditions for advancement
Owner: COO
4
READINESS DECISION
✓ Ready Now
Proceed to approval and promotion
◐ Ready Soon
Extend assessment 3 months with targeted development
✗ Not Yet Ready
Continue in current role; reassess in 6–12 months
Approval Chain
1–2 Weeks
  • COO recommendation memo to CEO/President
  • CEO review and approval
  • Board notification (if required by policy)
  • Compensation adjustment per HRF Compensation Framework
  • Updated job description and scope documentation
Owner: COO → CEO → Board (if applicable)
5
Announcement & Transition
Week of Promotion
  • Internal announcement with expanded scope framing
  • Team communication about any reporting changes
  • 90-day transition plan for new responsibilities
  • Updated systems, titles, and access
Owner: COO + HR
6
Post-Promotion Support
First 90 Days
  • Structured onboarding into VP scope
  • Executive coaching or peer mentoring (optional)
  • 30/60/90-day check-ins on transition progress
  • 6-month post-promotion review to confirm trajectory
Owner: COO + New VP
